# Volty (Smart Home Energy Management)

Android & Web Project built using flutter framework.

## LOGIC USED IN THIS PROJECT
. BloC State Management
. API Integration w/ PHP+MYSQL backend
. Shared & Secured Preferences
. Responsive Design
. Push Notifications
. WebSocket imitation
. AI Model

## ABOUT APP
. Smart home energy management
. Easily control & monitor your devices.
. In-detail energy reports & analytics with aid of AI.
. Multi-role access.
. Advanced notifications & alerts.
